## Description (4000 chars max)
When Deepblu shut down its servers, every COSMIQ+ dive computer lost its companion app. CosmiQ Companion brings yours back to life — as a fast, native iPhone app that talks directly to the dive computer over Bluetooth. No account. No cloud service that can disappear. Your dive computer, working again, for good.

FULL DEVICE CONTROL
Everything the original app could configure, right from your iPhone:
• Nitrox mix (21–40% O₂) and maximum PPO₂
• Safety factor: conservative, normal or progressive
• Scuba depth and time alarms
• Freedive max time and depth alarms
• High altitude and high salinity modes
• Units, date display, backlight, eco mode, screen timeout
• One-tap clock sync with your iPhone

Every change is written to the device and read back, so what you see is what your dive computer actually stored.

YOUR DIVES, BEAUTIFULLY LOGGED
• Download dives straight off the computer, with full depth and temperature profiles
• Interactive dive-profile charts
• Name your dives, add notes, and pin the dive site on a map — or tag it with your current location
• Fix a dive's date and time if the computer's clock was off
• Your logbook is stored on your iPhone and in your private iCloud Drive, so it survives reinstalling the app and follows you to your next phone

YOUR DATA IS YOURS
• Export any dive (or the whole logbook) as CSV or UDDF
• UDDF imports directly into Subsurface, MacDive, divelogs.de and more
• No account, no analytics, no tracking, no ads — the app makes zero network requests of its own
• Fully open source: github.com/pbouffaut/cosmiq-ios

BUILT ON COMMUNITY WORK
The COSMIQ Bluetooth protocol was reverse-engineered and shared by the diving community — the cosmiq5-web project and libdivecomputer (used by Subsurface). CosmiQ Companion builds on that work with love, and gives its own source code back.

COMPATIBILITY
Works with the Deepblu COSMIQ+ and Cosmiq Gen 5 dive computers over Bluetooth LE. Freedive depth alarms 3–6 are a Gen 5 hardware feature; the app automatically shows what your device supports.

SAFETY FIRST
This is an unofficial app, developed independently. Always verify settings on the dive computer's own screen before entering the water, and never rely on a single instrument for life-safety decisions. Dive safe — and enjoy having your COSMIQ back from the abyss.

CosmiQ Companion is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Deepblu, Inc. COSMIQ is a trademark of its respective owner; it is used here solely to describe device compatibility.

## App Privacy questionnaire
Select **"Data Not Collected"** for every category. Rationale: all data (dives,
settings, notes, locations) is stored on-device or in the user's own private
iCloud container; nothing is transmitted to the developer or any third party;
the app contains no analytics or tracking SDKs. Location is used only
on explicit user action and never leaves the device.
